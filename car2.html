It’s all about you.

You decide where your office is.

We have offices, of course, but there’s no obligation to work in
them. Based on the tasks in hand, each team decides for itself
whether office or remote work makes the most sense. We
support our employees with office spaces that suit their needs
and with state-of-the-art equipment that enables mobile work
from almost anywhere.

Our teams create ideas, not egos.

We can work better if we deal with each other openly and
appreciatively. We create a setting in which you can fully develop
your potential, while always remaining true to yourself. We listen
to each other, say what we think, and value every opinion:
including yours.

Lifelong learning is our standard
practice.

You can create new things with us and learn something new
every day. With our very own Academy for your further training
and the opportunity to gain experience at international
locations, we offer you the best conditions to grow even further.

Everyone wants to have more
sustainable mobility. We work on that
every day.

At Audi, we work every day on making our products and our
entire company even more sustainable. We consistently lower
our vehicles’ carbon emissions – from the purchase, through to
the driving time and the recycling stage. We invest in the
development of renewable energies. Our
aim is to make our plants fully carbon neutral by 2025.
